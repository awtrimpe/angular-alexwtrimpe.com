When I was in school, I lived in a fraternity that was very much not the popular organization on campus (at the time, we changed that) but I immediately wanted to work on our brand image when I joined. We really lacked in conveying a consistent and quality brand and having just taken my first computer science classes, I felt that I could help out with that, even if just in a small way. My first objective was to attack the website as our first line of attack for creating the brand. This is what the guys had originally made

While using fraternity colors is nice, that was a bit harsh on the eyes, so I felt we could go with something a little less intense on the eyes. I did not start with the above photo, but I wanted to include it to show you the roots of where our current design inspiration had blossomed from. This was the page I inherited.

I spent my first, whole winter break making this website so that we could have a nicer aesthetic coming into the new year. It is raw HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. It does make for less fun to maintain, but this was one of the first real projects I took on and taught me a lot. I am still proud of it, and still, maintain this site for the guys.
